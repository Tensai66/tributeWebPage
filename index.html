Neil deGrasse Tyson

  • 1958 - Neil deGrasse Tyson was born in Manhattan, New York City
  • 1967 - Trip to Hayden Planetarium at The Museum of History where he said that "[the] universe called to him" and he wanted to be an astronomer
  • 1976 - Graduated from Bronx Highschool of Science
  • 1980 - Graduated from Harvard University with a Bachelors in Physics
  • 1983 - Earned his Masters of Arts in Astronomy at University of Texas in Austin
  • 1991 - Earned his Doctorate of Philosophy in Astrophysics at Colombia University
  • 2001 - Appointed by George W. Bush onto the Future of the United States Aerospace Industry Commission
  • 2005 - Earned the NASA Distinguished Public Service Medal
